Data sources & honesty note

Surface: ACT LiDAR 2019 (0.5 m DTM). Shafts are placed by georeferenced OpenStreetMap coordinates, elevation-cross-checked against the LiDAR to within 0.5–2.5 m; per-shaft position/depth confidence varies (see each shaft's info panel). The qanat gallery is drawn near-level, per the site brochure's surveyed profile. Solid shafts have a documented depth; faded (translucent) shafts have an inferred depth.

  • Waringo/Faber/Werner (2018) — site brochure & survey
  • ACT LiDAR 2019 — 0.5 m digital terrain model
  • OpenStreetMap — qanat shaft node coordinates
  • EU-DEM 25 m — regional context only
