Data sources & honesty note

Surface: ACT LiDAR 2019 (0.5 m DTM). The main-line shafts (P‑1 to P9) are placed from a direct SIT survey (Henri Werner, LUREF/EPSG:2169 shaft centrepoints) — the highest-authority position in this project. The western/auxiliary shafts (P‑4/P‑5/P‑5A/P‑7A) aren't in that survey and stay on provisional OpenStreetMap coordinates, elevation-checked against the LiDAR to within 0.5–2.5 m. Per-shaft position confidence varies — see each shaft's info panel. The qanat gallery is drawn near-level, per the site brochure's surveyed profile. Solid shafts have a documented depth; faded (translucent) shafts have an inferred depth.

Elevations are the brochure's metres a.s.l.; the ACT LiDAR DTM (2019) is referenced to the Luxembourg height datum (NG95 / EVRF) and treated as consistent with the brochure's a.s.l. to within the stated 0.5–2.5 m.

  • ACT LiDAR 2019 — 0.5 m digital terrain model
  • OpenStreetMap — qanat shaft node coordinates
  • EU-DEM 25 m — regional context only
